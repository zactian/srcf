Where to Submit this Form

TRAVEL STUDY and INTERNSHIP requests must be submitted to Berkeley Summer Sessions.
If your request does NOT involve the Travel Study or Internship program, you may also submit the form to the Cal Student Central.

BERKELEY SUMMER SESSIONS
Submit in Person 1995 University Avenue (cross street Milvia)
Submit via Fax 510.664.9825
Submit via Email Completed form with original signature(s) must be scanned and attached to an email to summer@berkeley.edu with subject line RCF (insert your SID).
CAL STUDENT CENTRAL
Submit in Person 120 Sproul Hall

If these instructions are not followed, processing your request will be delayed.
